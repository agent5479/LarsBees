Powerful Features for Beekeepers

Everything you need to manage your apiary effectively

Location Tracking

Track all your hive sites with GPS coordinates. View them on interactive maps and manage multiple locations effortlessly.

Quick Action Logging

Log multiple tasks at once with simple checkboxes. Record inspections, feeding, treatments, and more in seconds.

Individual Hive Tracking

Monitor specific hives for infection management. Track status, add notes, and maintain detailed records.

Activity History

Keep comprehensive records of all hive activities. Archive old entries and maintain a clean, organized log.

Dashboard Analytics

Get an overview of your entire apiary at a glance. Track statistics, recent activities, and key metrics.

Custom Settings

Configure harvest timelines, sugar requirements, and custom notes for each hive site to fit your workflow.
